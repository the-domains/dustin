Who is Dustin W. Stout?

On Feb. 17, 1985 in small town Sharon, Pennsylvania I came into the world. I was born to a hard-working bartender mom and a regional rock-star dad.

Ever since as far as I can remember I’ve had aspirations of telling stories on the big screen. Although my mother did name me after famous actorDustin Hoffman, she never knew that I would some day pursue acting myself.
